About this Webapp

Sentinel allows its users to scrape tweets about a product or topic and gain valuable insights the help of Data Visualisation.


Sentinel uses Deeplearning NLP models to classify the sentiment of scraped tweets.


You can check out our source code on GitHub.

FAQs

Q. Why is it taking so long?

Sentinel scrapes tweets from Twitter and this process can take some time. Try reducing the time period or the number of tweets for a faster response.


Q. Are all tweets classified correctly?

Most of the tweets are classified correctly. But due to the intricacies of languages, we cannot say for certain if all tweets are classified correctly.
